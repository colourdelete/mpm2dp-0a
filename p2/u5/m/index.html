Minimum and Maximum

Note Not MikuMikuDance!

The minimum and maximum points of a quadratic relation is its vertex

For a quadratic relation in the form $$sy=a(x-h)^2+k$$e , the vertex is $s(h,k)$e and is also the maximum point of the parabola. The vertex is a minimum when $sa>0$e and maximum when $sa<0$e .
