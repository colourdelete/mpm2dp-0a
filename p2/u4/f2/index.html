Factor Quadratics 2

To factor trinomials in the form $sax^2+bx+c$e : $$sy+z=ac$$e $$syz=b$$e $$sax^2+bx+c$$e $$s=ax^2+yx+zx+c$$e

Example $$s6x^2+17x+12$$e Because $syz=ac$e , $sy+z=b$e , $sy=8$e and $sz=9$e . $$s=6x^2+8x+9x+12$$e $$s=2x(3x+4)+3(3x+4)$$e Then, factor by grouping. $$s=(2x+3)(3x+4)$$e
