In the zeroes form, {{<mi>}}y=a(x-r)(x-s){{</mi>}},
the x-intercepts are {{<mi>}}(r, 0){{</mi>}} and {{<mi>}}(s, 0){{</mi>}},
and the vertex is {{<mi>}}(\frac{r+s}{2}, y){{</mi>}}.
