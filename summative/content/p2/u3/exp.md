Negative exponents turn the number into its reciprocal, and
an exponent of {{<mi>}}0{{</mi>}} is always {{<mi>}}1{{</mi>}}.

{{<md>}}x^0=1{{</md>}}
{{<md>}}x^-1=\frac{1}{x}{{</md>}}

{{<eg id="lol">}}
{{<md>}}(\frac{2}{3})^{-2}=(\frac{3}{2})^2=\frac{9}{4}{{</md>}}
{{</eg>}}
