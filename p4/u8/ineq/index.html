Inequalities

When both sides of an inequality are multiplied or divided by a negative number, it is “reversed”. $$a\lt b$$ $$x\le y$$ $$-a\gt -b$$ $$-x\ge -y$$

For example, to solve $3x-2\le 2x+1$ and sketch it on the number line:

$$3x-2\le 2x+1$$ $$x\le 3$$
