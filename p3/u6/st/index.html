Trigonometry of Right Triangles

Congruent figures have the same size and shape.

Similar figures have the same shapebut different sizes.

The corresponding angles of similar figures are equal, and the ratios of the corresponding sides are equal, and is defined as the scale factor $sk$e .

Simiarly, the squares of the factors is the ratio of the areas.

The $s\sim$e is used to denote similarity.
